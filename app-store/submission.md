## Description

Absolute Timer is a focused iPhone training timer for boxing, MMA, HIIT, EMOM, and interval workouts.

Build reusable profiles for rounds, breaks, and total round count, then run each session with a large readable timer, clear color states, sound cues, voice announcements, and haptic feedback.

Features:
- Preset profiles for boxing, MMA, EMOM, and E2MOM.
- Custom profiles with configurable round duration, break duration, and round count.
- Large full-screen timer with active, break, paused, and idle states.
- Sound effects, voice announcements, and haptic feedback.
- On-device profile and settings storage.

Absolute Timer is a training timer only. It is not medical, safety, or coaching advice.
