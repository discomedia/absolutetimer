Absolute Timer Support

Support for Absolute Timer for iPhone.

Contact

Email support@discomedia.com.au for bugs, feedback, and feature requests.

Helpful Details

When reporting an issue, include:

  • Absolute Timer app version.
  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • The selected profile and timer settings.
  • Whether sound, voice announcements, or haptics were enabled.
  • Steps to reproduce the issue.

Privacy

Absolute Timer does not collect data. Profiles and settings stay on your device. Read the privacy policy.
