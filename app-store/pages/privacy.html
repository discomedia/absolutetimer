Absolute Timer Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Absolute Timer is a local training timer for iPhone. It does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

Data Stored On Device

The app stores timer profiles, selected profile, and sound, voice, and haptic settings on your device using Apple UserDefaults. This information stays on your device and is not sent to Discomedia or any third party.

Data Collection

Absolute Timer does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, accounts, push notifications, HealthKit, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or a backend service.

Training Disclaimer

Absolute Timer is a training timer only. It is not medical, safety, or coaching advice. Train within your limits and consult a qualified professional before beginning or changing an exercise program.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@discomedia.com.au.
